The folder marked Flying and Bombing Mode should sufficiently answer all 5 of the questions,
but if you wish to further inspect the placement of the models for questions 1 and 2, Free Camera Mode should be used.

Files should run in x86 mode.

Controls:

Free Camera Mode:
Q, E - Move camera down and up
A, D - Rotate camera left and right
W, S - Move camera forward and backward
Up, Down Arrows - Look up and down

Flying and Bombing Mode:
WASD - Move selected Speeder in a cardinal direction (can also move on diagonals)
P - Switch Speeder
Space - Toss a bomb
